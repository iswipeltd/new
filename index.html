OUR PROJECT ENGAGEMENT MODELS

IswipeTech offers multiple engagement models to clients, depending upon the project requirement’s nature, complexity, and budget. Our software developers are accessible for offshore and onshore engagement as per the client’s needs.

View all

Dedicated Resource Model

The customer can preview the resumes of our best pool of candidates to check their skill sets and precise experience that should match their project requirements.

Time & Material Model

This engagement model best fits when the scope of a project is open-ended. The customer pays only for the time invested and resources used on the project.

Team Hiring Model

The customer can hire a dedicated software development team, allowing him or his team to direct the hired team resources with full access and control.

Fixed-Cost Model

If the customer has a precise project story and scope ready, we offer them a fixed-cost model with a projected timeline. At times, it fits better when the customer has a tight budget as well.

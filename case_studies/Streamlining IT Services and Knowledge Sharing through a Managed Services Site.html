Streamlining IT Services and Knowledge Sharing through a Managed Services Site

Industry:
IT Services

Technology Used:

  • MOSS 2007
  • ASP.NET 2.0
  • C#
  • AJAX
  • SDE-BMC Services APIs
  • Tango/04 APIs

Client:

A Chicago-based IT Services company

Requirement:

    Create a site for an IT services company to manage client support and information sharing. The site should integrate with existing tools and streamline managed services.

Solution Delivered:

IswipeTech developed a comprehensive managed services site seamlessly integrated with existing tools like BMC Patrol Service Desk Express and Tango/04. The site provided clients with a user-friendly platform to access information, support documents, and request assistance. Custom workflows and event handlers were designed to optimize support processes, automate tasks, and ensure timely resolution of client inquiries. Integration with external systems facilitated seamless communication and data exchange, enhancing overall service delivery.

Results:

The client was able to get the following results –

  • Improved Service Management:Automated workflows and streamlined processes led to faster resolution times and improved service delivery.
  • Enhanced Client Support:A dedicated client portal provided a centralized platform for support requests, knowledge base access, and improved communication.
  • Streamlined Knowledge Sharing:Integration with existing tools facilitated efficient knowledge sharing and improved internal collaboration within the IT services team.
